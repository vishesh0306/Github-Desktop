About Us

We’ve Funded 20 Crore Rupees Over

Axis Educational Society is a non-profit organization that was established in 2008. Our mission is to promote rural development, provide quality education for children, and uplift girls in underprivileged communities. Over the past 12 years, we have been committed to making a positive impact on society. Our team of dedicated volunteers and staff work tirelessly to provide educational resources to children, empower women, and revitalize rural communities. We believe in providing a strong foundation for children to learn and grow, ensuring that they have access to a quality education. We recognize the importance of education in breaking the cycle of poverty and inequality.
